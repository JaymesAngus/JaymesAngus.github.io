This is my growing collection of notes from university courses, papers I've read, and various other sources. I began taking atomic notes using obsidian, an amazing note-taking resource, and have used a home-cooked python script to convert the original markdown files to html ones.

I can't guarantee that any of the information within these notes is 100% accurate, but I do my best to make sure it's at least somewhat correct!
